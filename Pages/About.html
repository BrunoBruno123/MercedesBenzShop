ABOUT US

The Mercedes-Benz Group can look back on a tradition that goes back to the inventors of the automobile – Gottlieb Daimler and Carl Benz – and features pioneering achievements in automotive engineering. The Group is one of the most successful automobile companies in the world and is one of the largest providers of high-end cars and premium vans. Financing, leasing, vehicle subscriptions and rental, fleet management, digital services for charging and payment, insurance brokerage and innovative mobility services round off the range of services
